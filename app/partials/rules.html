FAQ

Q1. What is Angels of Eureka and the Circle of Life about?
A. Angels of Eureka is dedicated to providing support to bereaved parents and families who are suffering as the result of the death of their child, children, mom, dad, sister, brother grandparent, etc...

Q. Why do you call lost loved ones "Angels"?
A. We think this is a cute way to refer to your loved ones without highlighting the sadness of your loss unnecessarily.

Q. Is there a charge for the memorial walls?
A. This is a free service

Q. What is an Angel Anniversary?
A. These are dates that are important to the legacy of your Angel. Share a memory with all his/her loved ones, and celebrate their life and encourage one another!

Q. What do you offer as the features of memorial walls?
A. Memorial walls feature timelines, photo galleries, videos and Angel Anniversary events.
Angel's Timeline: See all the current topics, photos and events about your Angel in one convenient place. Share a memory for all of his/her friends and family to enjoy.
Photos: Share photos, and organize them into galleries. You can also upload your own Imikimi montage creations!
Videos:Share videos of your Angel. You add either youtube or Animoto videos!
Angel Anniversaries: Specify a date that is important to the legacy of your Angel. Share a memory with all his/her loved ones, and celebrate their life and encourage one another!

Q. Are the memorial walls only for humans?
A. No, you can create a memorial wall for your beloved pet as well!
The loss of a pet can deeply affect a person spiritually, emotionally and physically. Pets are our faithful friends, providing loyalty and companionship on good days and bad days. Pets comfort us, and when we lose these cherished companions, the compassion and sympathy we need for dealing with their loss. So please feel free to share stories and photos about your beloved pet.

Q. Can I create private support groups?
A.Yes, We offer private groups that have the same features as the memorial walls.

Q. Is this safe and secure?
A. Safety of our community is our utmost concern. If you see anything suspicous on this site, contact Site Administration immediately so we can take care of it.

Thank you for visiting Angels of Eureka!

Be Blessed,

Dana Novak, Patricia Wilson, Greg Wilson and Staff!
